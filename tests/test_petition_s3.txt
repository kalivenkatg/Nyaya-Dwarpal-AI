IN THE MATTER OF:

PETITIONER: Rajesh Kumar
VERSUS
RESPONDENT: State of Delhi

PETITION UNDER ARTICLE 226 OF THE CONSTITUTION OF INDIA

FACTS:

1. The petitioner is a resident of Delhi and a law-abiding citizen.

2. On 15th January 2024, the petitioner was wrongfully detained by the police without any warrant or justification under IPC Section 302.

3. The petitioner was not informed of the grounds of arrest as required under CrPC Section 154.

4. The petitioner seeks immediate relief from this illegal detention.

GROUNDS:

A. The detention violates Article 21 of the Constitution of India.

B. The police failed to follow proper procedure under IPC Section 307.

C. No FIR was filed as required under CrPC Section 161.

PRAYER:

WHEREFORE, in light of the facts and grounds stated above, the petitioner most respectfully prays that this Hon'ble Court may be pleased to:

a) Issue a writ of habeas corpus directing the respondent to produce the petitioner before this Court;

b) Declare the detention of the petitioner as illegal and unconstitutional;

c) Direct the respondent to pay compensation to the petitioner for illegal detention;

d) Pass any other order that this Hon'ble Court deems fit in the interest of justice.

AND FOR THIS ACT OF KINDNESS, THE PETITIONER SHALL DUTY BOUND FOREVER PRAY.
